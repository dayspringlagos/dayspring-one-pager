Dayspring Lagos is a branch of Believers Way of Salvation Ministry, committed to raising believers who live right, grow strong, and influence their world through Kingdom truth.

Who We Are

Dayspring Lagos was launched on November 3, 2024, in Surulere, Lagos, as a vibrant expression of Believers Way of Salvation Ministry.

We exist to equip believers to discover, understand, and renew their minds through sound doctrine, discipleship, prayer, and community.

Core Values (REAL)

  • Excellence
  • Accountability
  • Love
